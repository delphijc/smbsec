# AI Enabled Blue Team Operations for Small to Medium Size Businesses

*A practical guide to implementing AI-powered blue team security operations in SMB environments*

## About This Book

This book provides a comprehensive guide to leveraging artificial intelligence for cybersecurity operations in small to medium-sized businesses. It bridges the gap between enterprise-level AI security solutions and the practical needs of resource-constrained SMB security teams.
